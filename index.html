FORGED FROM SCARLET CRIMSON ORE

Nichirin Blades

Each blade changes color upon first being drawn — a reflection of the demon slayer's very soul and breathing style.

The Legend of the Nichirin

Forged from Scarlet Crimson Iron Sand collected from high mountain ore deposits that are constantly bathed in sunlight, Nichirin blades are the only weapons capable of permanently slaying demons. The ore absorbs the power of the sun — the ultimate weakness of all demonic beings.

  • Engraved with "Destroyer of Demons" (Akki Messatsu)
  • Color reflects the wielder's breathing style
  • Unique tsuba (handguard) for each slayer
  • Turn crimson-red when a Hashira reaches their peak

THE NINE PILLARS

The Hashira

The strongest demon slayers in existence — nine elite warriors who are the last line of defense between humanity and the demonic hordes of Muzan Kibutsuji.

Second only to Master Kagaya Ubuyashiki

Gyomei Himejima

Stone Hashira — 岩柱

Widely acknowledged as the strongest Hashira — a towering, gentle giant who is completely blind yet possesses unmatched physical power. His weapon is a flail and axe, not a conventional sword. His tears flow constantly, but his heart is iron.

Stone BreathingFlail & AxeDemon Slayer Mark

Sanemi Shinazugawa

Wind Hashira — 風柱

Fierce, aggressive, and battle-hardened. Sanemi bears countless scars from years of fighting demons and possesses rare Marechi blood — so intoxicating to demons that it paralyzes them. His fury drives him to the absolute limit in every battle.

Wind BreathingMarechi BloodScarred Veteran

Giyu Tomioka

Water Hashira — 水柱

The first Hashira encountered by Tanjiro — stoic, solitary, and extraordinarily skilled. Giyu rarely shows emotion but acts on a strict internal code of justice. He created a unique 11th form of Water Breathing that no one else knows.

Water Breathing11th Form (Unique)Half-and-Half Haori

Kyojuro Rengoku

Flame Hashira — 炎柱

Radiant, passionate, and utterly devoted. Rengoku's burning spirit inspires everyone around him. He faces the Upper Rank Three demon Akaza on the Mugen Train in one of the most celebrated battles in anime history — and never once loses his smile.

Flame BreathingMugen Train ArcBlazing Spirit

Obanai Iguro

Serpent Hashira — 蛇柱

Cold, meticulous and unforgiving of weakness — yet fiercely devoted to Mitsuri Kanroji. His twisted blade mimics the sinuous path of a serpent. He is always accompanied by his white snake, Kaburamaru, who acts as his eyes during battle.

Serpent BreathingTwisted BladeKaburamaru

Mitsuri Kanroji

Love Hashira — 恋柱

Warm, emotional, and deceptively powerful. Mitsuri possesses eight times the muscle density of a normal person, granting her explosive strength. Her whip-like blade and Love Breathing style are perfectly tuned to her exceptionally flexible body.

Love BreathingHyper-Dense MusclesWhip Blade

Muichiro Tokito

Mist Hashira — 霞柱

A child prodigy who achieved Hashira status in just two months — an unprecedented feat. Muichiro initially appears emotionless and detached due to amnesia, but awakens fearsome power when he reconnects with his past and the memory of his twin brother.

Mist BreathingChild ProdigyDemon Slayer Mark

Tengen Uzui

Sound Hashira — 音柱

A flamboyant ex-shinobi of the Uzui clan with three kunoichi wives. Tengen wields two massive cleavers on chains loaded with explosive blades. His Sound Breathing lets him read a demon's "score" — transforming a battle into a musical composition.

Sound BreathingShinobi OriginTwin Cleavers

Shinobu Kocho

Insect Hashira — 蟲柱

Seemingly cheerful and gentle — but Shinobu harbors a deep, cold rage beneath her smile. Unable to behead demons due to lack of strength, she instead stores lethal Wisteria poison in her blade, injecting fatal doses with razor-precise jabs.

THOSE WHO CAME BEFORE

Former Hashira Legends

Before the nine pillars, there were others. Their stories are written in sacrifice, grief, and the enduring will to protect humanity at any cost.

MENTOR — RETIRED

Sakonji Urokodaki

Former Water Hashira

The masked guardian of Mt. Sagiri — Urokodaki shaped an entire generation of demon slayers. His red tengu mask hides a face weathered by decades of loss. He trained Giyu Tomioka and took Tanjiro Kamado under his wing, becoming a surrogate father to both.

Urokodaki felt personally responsible for every student killed by the Hand Demon — a demon who hunted down his former pupils. He placed curses on the demon as a final act of protection for Tanjiro during the Selection.

"If Tanjiro fails to kill the demon… I will commit seppuku out of shame."
Water Breathing GrandmasterTengu MaskMt. Sagiri
TRAGEDY — RETIRED AT 35

Jigoro Kuwajima

Former Thunder Hashira

Sharp-eyed and demanding, "Gramps" trained two students — Zenitsu Agatsuma and Kaigaku. He lost his leg in battle and was forced to retire at age 35, but channeled all remaining passion into raising the next Thunder Hashira.

When his senior student Kaigaku willingly became a demon to survive, Jigoro's shame became unbearable. He committed ritual suicide — seppuku — taking responsibility for his student's betrayal. Zenitsu never forgave Kaigaku for this.

"Every master passes on their everything to their student. What Kaigaku did… was an unforgivable betrayal of that bond."
Thunder BreathingZenitsu's MasterDied of Seppuku
GRIEF & REDEMPTION

Shinjuro Rengoku

Former Flame Hashira

Father of Kyojuro and Senjuro, Shinjuro was once a proud and fiery Hashira who embodied the blazing spirit of Flame Breathing. But the death of his beloved wife shattered him, and the discovery of the Flame Breathing scrolls — revealing that Sun Breathing was superior — broke what remained.

He became a bitter alcoholic recluse, belittling his sons, abandoning his duty. Only when Tanjiro brought him news of Kyojuro's final moments — and his dying message of love and pride — did Shinjuro begin to confront his grief and find his way back.

"Kyojuro… you were always the best of us."
Flame BreathingRengoku FamilyPath of Redemption
FALLEN AT 17 — NEVER FORGOTTEN

Kanae Kocho

Former Flower Hashira

Kanae was the radiant older sister who gave Shinobu Kocho her smile — and her grief. Unlike any other Hashira, Kanae genuinely believed that humans and demons could one day coexist. Her compassion was boundless, even in the face of monsters.

That belief was tested when she encountered Upper Rank Two — Doma, the very demon who would murder her. She fought until dawn, striking him nine times, but her body failed. She died at age 17 — just as the sun rose to save her — passing the Butterfly Mansion and her mantle to Shinobu.

"I hope one day… demons and humans can live together in peace. Even after I am gone."
Flower BreathingButterfly MansionKilled by Doma (UR2)
RETIRED — STILL SERVING

Tengen Uzui

Former Sound Hashira (Retired)

After the catastrophic battle in the Entertainment District, Tengen lost his left eye and right hand fighting alongside Tanjiro against Upper Rank Six — the sibling pair Daki and Gyutaro. Despite defeating two Upper Rank demons simultaneously, the toll on his body was too great to continue active duty.

Tengen retired from the Hashira lineup, but never truly left the Corps. During the Hashira Training Arc, he led grueling physical conditioning courses for new slayers — proving that the flashiest Hashira had far more depth than his showmanship suggested.

"Whether I live or die — it will be in the most flamboyant way possible."

TWELVE KIZUKI

Upper Rank Demons

Born from human despair and empowered by Muzan's blood — these are the most terrifying beings in existence, each having slaughtered Hashira throughout history.

♔

Muzan Kibutsuji

The Demon King — 鬼舞辻無惨

The progenitor of all demons and the embodiment of evil. Muzan killed Tanjiro's family and turned Nezuko into a demon, setting the entire story in motion. He seeks immunity to sunlight to become a truly perfect being — and annihilates anyone who speaks his name. His cells can create new demons, evolve existing ones, or destroy them on a whim. No demon acts without his will.

Upper Rank I

Kokushibo

Moon Breathing

Once a human — the twin brother of Yoriichi Tsugikuni, the greatest demon slayer who ever lived. Envious of his brother's divine gift for Sun Breathing and terrified of death, he chose demonhood. He carries the tragic irony of having become the ultimate monster while seeking to surpass a saint. His Moon Breathing has sixteen forms and creates crescent-shaped blades from his very flesh.

Moon Breathing (16 Forms) Former Demon Slayer Yoriichi's Twin Multiple Eyes
Upper Rank II

Doma

Cryogenic Blood Demon Art

Doma is perhaps the most disturbing villain in the series — a hollow, empty entity who has never experienced genuine emotion, yet perfectly mimics warmth and devotion. He leads a cult of followers he ultimately devours. His ice-based Blood Demon Art creates blizzards and frozen carnage. He murdered Kanae Kocho and absorbed Shinobu Kocho — eventually meeting his end from her wisteria poison stored within her body.

Ice Blood Demon Art Killed Kanae Kocho Emotionally Hollow Cult Leader
Upper Rank III

Akaza

Destructive Death — Martial Arts

A pure martial artist of terrifying power — Akaza fights entirely with his fists and a Compass Needle technique that reads opponents' fighting spirit. He despises weakness and kills the strong out of respect. His tragic human backstory as Hakuji — a boy who trained desperately to save those he loved — makes him one of the most complex demons. He personally defeats Rengoku on the Mugen Train.

Destructive Death Art Defeated Rengoku Hateful of Weakness No Weapons
Upper Rank IV

Hantengu

Emotion-Split Clones

Hantengu's Blood Demon Art manifests his emotions as powerful individual demon clones — each representing fear, anger, pleasure, sorrow, and joy. His true self, Zohakuten, emerges when the clones merge. Despite his grotesque power, Hantengu himself is a coward who weeps and screams that he is innocent of all sin.

Emotion Split Art Clone Manifestation Zohakuten Form Cowardly True Form
Upper Rank V

Gyokko

Vase Blood Demon Art

A narcissistic demon who considers himself a supreme artist, Gyokko's Blood Demon Art involves emerging from decorative vases and creating grotesque sculptures from the bodies of his victims. He can spawn fish demons and move between vases instantly. His true form is a hideous aquatic monstrosity that sheds its human-like skin.

Vase Teleportation Fish Demon Spawn Narcissist Artist True Form: Aquatic
Upper Rank VI

Daki & Gyutaro

Sash & Sickle Blood Demon Arts

A pair of siblings sharing a single Upper Rank — Daki's beautiful exterior hides in Gyutaro's body. Their tragic backstory as starving children in the Yoshiwara district makes them sympathetic monsters. Gyutaro is viciously intelligent and deeply protective of Daki. Together they were almost unstoppable — until Tanjiro, Zenitsu, Inosuke, and Tengen decapitated them simultaneously.

Notable Demon

Rui

Thread Blood Demon Art — Lower Rank V

Rui is a lonely demon who desperately craved the family bonds he lost as a human. He created a false "family" on Mt. Natagumo by forcing other demons into roles of father, mother, and siblings — punishing them brutally when they failed. His steel-wire threads are nearly invisible and can slice through almost anything. Tanjiro's first encounter with a Twelve Kizuki.

Steel Thread ArtMt. Natagumo Lower Rank VCraved Family
Notable Demon

Enmu

Dream Manipulation — Lower Rank I

The last surviving Lower Rank, Enmu was granted extra blood by Muzan himself after displaying sadistic loyalty. His Blood Demon Art traps victims in deep, blissful dreams while destroying their spiritual cores. He fused with the Mugen Train itself, making the entire locomotive his body. The main antagonist of the Mugen Train arc.
